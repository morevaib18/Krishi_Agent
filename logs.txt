My crop has yellow spots | Recommended crop: Soybean for Maharashtra.

Yellow spots on your crop indicate a fungal infection. Here are some steps to address this issue:

1. Remove any infected plants to prevent the spread of the fungus.
2. Apply a fungicide recommended for soybean crops to control the fungal infection.
3. Ensure proper air circulation around the plants to reduce humidity, which can promote fungal growth.
4. Water the plants at the base to avoid wetting the leaves, as moisture on the leaves can also contribute to fungal infections.

Safety Note: When applying fungicides, wear appropriate protective gear such as gloves, goggles, and a mask to protect yourself from exposure to chemicals.
