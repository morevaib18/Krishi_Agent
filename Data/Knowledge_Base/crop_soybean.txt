Soybean Crop Guide

Climate:
- Suitable for warm and humid climate
- Ideal temperature: 20°C to 30°C

Soil:
- Well-drained loamy soil is best

Sowing Season:
- Kharif season (June–July)

Water Requirement:
- Moderate rainfall required

Fertilizer:
- Use NPK fertilizer
- Add organic compost

Common Issues:
- Yellow leaves may indicate fungal infection

Best Regions:
- Maharashtra, Madhya Pradesh

Advice:
- Avoid waterlogging
- Ensure proper spacing
